ABOUT

My name is Erick Ramos and I'm a junior at Mercy University studying graphic design, focusing on motion and branding. Ever since I was little, I've always had a kick for expressing myself through creation. For the last few years, I found my true potential in design, and I aspire to bring my ideas to fruition through typography and motion graphics. When I'm not designing, I love to paint, watch films, or play video games. Let's work!
